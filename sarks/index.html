What is DNS?

Domain Name System(DNS) is similar to your phonebook directory. It converts a domain name to an IP address which your browser uses to locate and display the information requested.

  • Your Internet service provider(ISP) is most likely your DNS provider meaning that they can see what you do and every site you visit.
  • Your activity traces can be used to target you with ads.
  • No protection against Malware or Phishing sites.
  • No privacy protection.

What can Sarks Sinkhole do?

Sarks Sinkhole can block ads and custom URL lists and offer protection against Malware or Phishing sites all while maintaining privacy.

  • Scale on-demand with traffic surge protection
  • Automatic daily updates
  • Full visibility via Developer Console
